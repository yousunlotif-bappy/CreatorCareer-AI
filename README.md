# CreatorCareer AI

## From Content Creator to Digital Entrepreneur

CreatorCareer AI is an AI-powered creator business intelligence platform built for the July Challenge: Reimagine Creative Industries with AI.

## Problem Statement

Many content creators can create videos, posts, or short-form content, but they struggle to turn their audience into a sustainable digital business. They often lack guidance on product validation, monetization strategy, audience fit, ethical promotion, and business roadmap planning.

## Solution

CreatorCareer AI helps creators generate AI-powered content packages, analyze market opportunities, validate product ideas, create affiliate and dropshipping roadmaps, check ethical monetization risks, and download a business roadmap report.

## MVP Features

1. Dashboard
2. Creator Profile Setup
3. AI Content Package Generator
4. AI Market Analysis
5. Product Validation Score
6. Affiliate & Dropshipping Roadmap
7. Ethical Monetization Checker
8. 7-Agent Creator Business Dashboard
9. Downloadable PDF Business Report

## Technology Stack

- Frontend: Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS
- Backend: Python FastAPI
- AI Model: IBM Granite / watsonx
- Development Tool: IBM Bob
- Database: Supabase / PostgreSQL
- Report System: PDF generation
