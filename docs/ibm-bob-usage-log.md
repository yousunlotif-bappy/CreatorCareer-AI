## Day 3: Creator Profile Setup

IBM Bob was used to plan and refine the Creator Profile Setup module, FastAPI /profile/analyze endpoint, frontend form structure, response fields, and UI workflow.

The Day 3 module collects creator niche, platform, followers, audience, region, skills, business interest, income goal, available time, and current challenge. The backend returns creator stage, readiness score, positioning, business opportunity, recommended modules, and next best action.


## Day 4: AI Content Package Generator

IBM Bob was used to plan and refine the Content Package Generator module, frontend form, FastAPI `/content/package` endpoint, prompt-style output structure, and contest-ready creative workflow.

This module helps creators turn one content topic into titles, hooks, scripts, captions, hashtags, thumbnail text, CTA, and platform-specific content notes. 



## Day 5: AI Market Analysis

IBM Bob was used to plan and refine the AI Market Analysis module, sample market signal structure, FastAPI `/market/analyze` endpoint, frontend market analysis form, opportunity scoring, product idea grouping, and business-focused result cards.

This module helps creators identify estimated market opportunities based on niche, audience, region, platforms, business type, budget, and product interest.


## Day 6: Product Validation Score

IBM Bob was used to plan and refine the Product Validation module, scoring formula, FastAPI `/product/validate` endpoint, frontend form, validation checklist, and business recommendation output.

This module helps creators validate whether a product idea fits their niche, audience, platform, content style, business model, and launch readiness.



## Day 7: Affiliate & Dropshipping Roadmap

IBM Bob was used to plan and refine the Affiliate & Dropshipping Roadmap module, FastAPI `/affiliate/roadmap` endpoint, frontend form, roadmap output structure, content promotion plan, supplier research checklist, risk analysis, and ethical disclosure reminder.

This module helps creators choose validation-first affiliate and dropshipping opportunities based on niche, audience, platform, region, budget, product category, and content style.


## Day 8: Ethical Monetization Checker

IBM Bob was used to plan and refine the Ethical Monetization Checker module, FastAPI `/ethical/check` endpoint, risk detection logic, frontend form, safer rewrite output, affiliate disclosure workflow, and responsible monetization guidance.

This module helps creators check promotional captions, affiliate scripts, and product claims before publishing.



## Day 9: 7-Agent Creator Business Dashboard

IBM Bob was used to plan and refine the 7-Agent Creator Business Dashboard, FastAPI `/agents/run` endpoint, multi-agent scoring logic, frontend dashboard UI, unified business opportunity score, executive summary, and next 7 days action plan.

This module runs seven creator business agents together to help creators move from content creation to digital entrepreneurship using validation-first and ethical monetization guidance.


## Day 10: PDF Business Report Generator

IBM Bob was used to plan and refine the PDF Business Report Generator, FastAPI `/report/generate` endpoint, ReportLab PDF structure, frontend report page, report saving workflow, and download workflow.

This module converts CreatorCareer AI analysis into a downloadable business report with profile summary, scores, 7-agent analysis, next 7 days plan, 6-month roadmap, ethical note, and MVP data note.
