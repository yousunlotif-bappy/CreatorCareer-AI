# CreatorCareer AI - MVP Scope

## Project Name

CreatorCareer AI

## Tagline

From Content Creator to Digital Entrepreneur

## Contest Theme

Reimagine Creative Industries with AI

## Main Goal

CreatorCareer AI helps content creators turn their content skills into a digital business using AI-powered content generation, market analysis, product validation, affiliate/dropshipping roadmap planning, ethical monetization checking, and a 7-agent creator business dashboard.

## MVP Features

1. Dashboard
2. Creator Profile Setup
3. AI Content Package Generator
4. AI Market Analysis
5. Product Validation Score
6. Affiliate & Dropshipping Roadmap
7. Ethical Monetization Checker
8. 7-Agent Creator Business Dashboard
9. Downloadable PDF Business Report

## Future Scope

These features will not be built in the first MVP:

- Story-to-video planner
- Voiceover optimizer
- Content repurpose engine
- Advanced analytics
- Team collaboration
- Live marketplace API integration

## Winning Positioning

CreatorCareer AI is not just a content generator. It is an AI-powered creator business intelligence platform that helps creators move from content creation to digital entrepreneurship.
